NYSC ORIENTATION CAMPS

Prospective corps members are passed through the orientation exercise.
This is a combination of para-military activities and organized regimented training for 21 days. It is the very first phase of the SERVICE YEAR.
After you have collected your Call-Up letter, you are expected to report to the orientation camp.
